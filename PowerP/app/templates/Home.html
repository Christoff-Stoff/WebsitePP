Power Partners

Innovative and simple solutions for complicated engineering problems

Power Partners Group was established in 2019. Power Partners provides safe and profitable engineering and renewable energy solutions through its dynamic approach whilst building and maintaining strategic relationships with its clients. The Power Partners team has over two decades of combined experience in building and renewable energy services.

Power Partners provides high quality engineering services in the building environment which include electrical engineering, electronic engineering and renewable energy solutions.

Power Partners also owns and operates long-term commercial solar photovoltaic (“PV”) installations with direct electricity off-take agreements. Installations include roof and ground mounted structures.

What we Offer

Power Partners offers services in the following industries:

Electrical Services

The delivery of our consulting services and our accurate, value-adding designs is founded on the strength of the professional relationships between our firm and our clients. These enable us to walk the extra mile for our clients. Our dynamic electrical engineering team has developed highly accurate models which provide our clients with precise designs in support of:

  • feasibility studies
  • power reticulation
  • load calculations and
  • lux level simulations and energy models.

Electronic Services

Our electronic designs are integrated with our electrical projects to ensure effective and efficient collaboration between both services. Our electronic services include:

  • fire detection and alarms;
  • voice evacuation systems;
  • audio visual and background music;
  • structured cabling systems;
  • PABX and communication systems;
  • security and CCTV systems;
  • building management systems; and
  • smart buildings (IoT).
